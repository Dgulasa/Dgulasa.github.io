Practical stuff

Where to stay in Cardiff

There are several small hotels on Cathedral Road, which is just a few mins walk from both venues for the ceremony and the garden party. The city-centre is around 20-25 mins walk away - and there is a wide selection of hotels across Cardiff centre.

Getting to Cardiff

If you are travelling from further afield, it might be useful to know that Cardiff has an international airport - but it is rather small and doesn't fly direct to many destinations. Bristol Airport is around 1hr by car (or 1hr30 by public transport) away - and has many Easyjet destinations. London Airports can all be reached easily by public transport from Cardiff - but take longer to get to. The train link Cardiff to London Paddington is only 2 hours - but can be rather expensive. The 'Megabus' is the cheapest way to get to/from London - but takes at least 4 hours.

What else to do while you're here

Wales is a beautiful country - so if you have time to add a few days to your trip, we'd highly recommend you visit the spectacular beaches on the Gower (Three Cliffs Bay and Rhossili especially). For quite different scenery, you can also head inland to the Brecon Beacons.
